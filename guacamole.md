# Guacamole

## Ingredients
* 3 ripe avocados
* 1 lemon, juiced
* 1/2 teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
* 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
* 1/4 cup finely diced red onion
* 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
* 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
* Pinch of cayenne pepper

## Instructions
1. Halve and pit the avocados, then scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
2. Add the lemon juice and salt, then mash until the guacamole is as chunky or smooth as you like.
3. Fold in the tomatoes, red onion, jalapeño, cilantro, garlic, cumin, and cayenne pepper.
4. Taste and adjust with more salt, lemon juice, or cayenne for extra zing.
5. Serve immediately with tortilla chips, tacos, or fresh vegetables.
